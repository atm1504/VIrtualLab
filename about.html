About Us

Virtual Lab for Engineering Practicum

We a group of five people from IIT Patna Mechanical Engineering have started developing this site under Engineering Practicum Course of our Academic Curriculum. We present to you all details about simple and double Pendulum in as much details as possible. We have written down animation codes in python and matlab to present you all an animation of the motion of the pendulums. The website is still in developing phase, the features int his site would be enhanced over the time.
